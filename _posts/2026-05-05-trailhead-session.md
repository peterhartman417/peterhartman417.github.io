# Trailhead Session

**Founding client rate: $50 for a limited time**  
**45 minutes**  
**Owner-present private training in the Nixa area**

Every good adventure starts at the trailhead.

The Trailhead Session is a 45-minute owner-present training session for active dogs and owners who need a clear path forward.

We will look at what is actually happening with your dog, choose the first problem to solve, and start building a practical training path.

This is a good fit if your dog:

- pulls on leash
- jumps on people
- gets overexcited outside the house
- ignores you in public
- has lots of energy and not enough direction
- makes walks, parks, or everyday outings harder than they should be

You will leave with:

- a clearer picture of what your dog needs
- simple exercises to practice at home
- a recommended next step
- book or tool recommendations when useful
- a training path that fits your dog instead of a generic checklist

Training takes place at a Nixa-area public location selected by Bring The Dog Training. I choose the location based on the dog, the training goal, and safety.

You stay with your dog for the entire session.

No drop-off. No daycare. No boarding. No dog transport.

Start at the Trailhead and let us pick the path for your dog.
